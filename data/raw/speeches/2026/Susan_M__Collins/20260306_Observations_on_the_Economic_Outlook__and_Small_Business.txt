Federal Reserve Bank of Boston President & CEO Susan M. Collins delivered remarks on the economic outlook and small business conditions at Outlook 2026, hosted by the Springfield Regional Chamber of Commerce in Springfield, Massachusetts.
Collins discussed economic conditions, highlighted the challenges and opportunities facing small businesses, and emphasized the value of the Federal Reserve’s federated structure.
