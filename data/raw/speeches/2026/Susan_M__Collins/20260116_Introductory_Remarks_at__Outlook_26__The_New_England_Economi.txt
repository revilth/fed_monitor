Federal Reserve Bank of Boston President & CEO Susan M. Collins delivered brief, opening remarks and introduced the keynote speaker, Michelle W. Bowman, Vice Chair for Supervision at the Federal Reserve Board, at "
Outlook 26: The New England Economic Forum
."
The event, hosted by the six New England Bankers Associations, is a forum for bankers and business leaders that focuses on sharing economic perspectives and insights.
