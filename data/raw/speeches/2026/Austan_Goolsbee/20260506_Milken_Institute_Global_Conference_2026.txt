Austan Goolsbee, president and CEO of the Chicago Fed, will participate in a panel discussion on May 6 at the 2026 Milken Institute Global Conference in Los Angeles, California. Benedict Evans, tech analyst and venture partner at Mosaic Ventures, Sebastian Mallaby, senior fellow at the Council on Foreign Relations, and Catherine Wood, CEO and chief investment officer of ARK Invest, will also be on the panel. Moderated by Wall Street Journal columnist James Mackintosh, the discussion will begin at 12:00 pm CT and livestream from this webpage.
