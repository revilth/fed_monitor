Austan Goolsbee, president and CEO of the Chicago Fed, will participate in a panel discussion on May 8 at the 2026 Hoover Institution Monetary Policy Conference in Stanford, California. Federal Reserve Vice Chair for Supervision Michelle Bowman, Federal Reserve Governor Christopher Waller, and San Francisco Fed President Mary Daly will also sit on the panel. Moderated by Paola Sapienza, J-P Conte Family senior fellow at Hoover, the discussion will livestream from this webpage at 6:30 pm CT.
