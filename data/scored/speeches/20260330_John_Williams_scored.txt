INFLATION

  → Williams: "Inflation remains somewhat elevated due to the
    effects of tariffs, but those effects should begin to
    dissipate later this year."

Own language — KEY additions vs. Mar 3:
  "The significant increase in energy prices resulting from
   developments in the Middle East will likely boost overall
   inflation in coming months, but these effects should
