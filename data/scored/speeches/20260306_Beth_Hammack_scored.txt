INFLATION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Own language — KEY:
  "Currently, US inflation is too high and has been above our
   objective for the past five years." — DURATION FRAMING: same
   "five years" language as Barkin Jan 6 and Logan May 1. Hammack
   adopts this exact formulation, making it a 3-speaker phrase.
  "The latest PCE inflation reading was 2.9 percent in December 2025."
  "I see inflationary pressures as broad based. Tariffs are only one

    This directly contradicts the look-through framing used by
    Williams, Jefferson, Paulson, Bowman, and Waller's Jan 30
    dissent. Hammack's inflation view is structurally hawkish.

MONETARY POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Own language — KEY:
  "Given this combination [elevated inflation + labor softening]
