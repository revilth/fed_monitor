INFLATION

Own language — KEY:
  "The inflation hasn't been ideal. Has at least stalled out at

    more alarmed than the committee's standard "somewhat elevated."

  On energy/oil:

   the inflation side of the mandate getting worse. At the same
   time, the employment side of the mandate is getting worse.
   And that's always the worst case scenario for the central bank
   because there's not an obvious monetary policy answer to a
   stagflationary shock."
  → EARLIEST EXPLICIT STAGFLATION NAMING in this scoring set.
